Dataset Information

Project: ESP32-CAM At-Home Object Detection

Classes:
1. Background
2. Water bottle
3. Xbox controller

Dataset:
- Approximately 75 background images
- Approximately 75 water bottle images
- Approximately 75 Xbox controller images

Collection environment:
Images were collected in an at-home setting using different backgrounds, lighting conditions, camera angles, distances, and object positions.

Purpose:
The dataset was used to train an Edge Impulse object detection model that can identify a water bottle and an Xbox controller.
